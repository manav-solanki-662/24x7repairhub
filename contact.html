Our Services

24x7 Repair Hub Service Center assists customers with repair, maintenance, and installation needs for refrigerators, washing machines, televisions, air conditioners, and other home appliances. We help arrange appointments with skilled technicians for in-home service.

  • Appliance Repair & Maintenance: We help schedule technicians who can diagnose issues and repair most major home appliances. You will receive a clear explanation of the issue and a quotation before work begins.
  • New Appliance Installation: Technicians can assist with installation or setup of new appliances if required. Availability may vary depending on the appliance and location.
  • Diagnostic Visits: If the issue is unclear, a technician will inspect the appliance at your home and provide a quotation for the recommended repair or replacement. You are free to accept or decline the quotation.

How We Work

When you contact us, our support team will collect your basic details and schedule a visit. A technician will then inspect the appliance and provide a transparent estimate for repair. No online advance payment is required—payment is made directly to the technician after the service.
